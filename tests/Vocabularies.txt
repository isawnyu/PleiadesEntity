Availability of Expected Vocabularies
=====================================

Get portal vocabulary tool
--------------------------

    >>> vt = self.portal.portal_vocabularies
    
Test place types vocabulary
---------------------------

    >>> v = vt.getVocabularyByName('AWMCPlaceTypes')
    >>> v.Title()
    'Types of Places Distinguished in AWMC Publications'
    >>> len([obj for obj in v.contentValues() if obj.meta_type == "VdexTerm"])
    51

Test ancient name languages vocabulary
---------------------------

    >>> v = vt.getVocabularyByName('AWMCAncientNameLanguages')
    >>> v.Title()
    'Standard Codes for Languages and Writing Systems of Ancient Names'
    >>> len([obj for obj in v.contentValues() if obj.meta_type == "VdexTerm"])
    4
